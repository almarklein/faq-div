Does FAQ-this work in narrow content too?

Yes, behold the current page!

What about mobile divices?

The FAQ scales along nicely on devices with small screens, and if you want to tweak the behavior, you can just use CSS!

How does the FAQ scale on wide screens?

The elements in the FAQ have a max width of 800px. You can of course change this with CSS by changing .faq > .qa and faq > .search.
